Daniel
          Faour.

Master's student at the University in Oslo with main focus in UX-design and web development.

I am currently pursuing a master's degree in Informatics: Design, Use, and Interaction at the Institute for Informatics, part of the University of Oslo's newer departments.

Ever since I was a young boy, I have been captivated by technology and its impact on the world around me. Over the years, I have grown into someone who thoroughly enjoys working with computers. You could say that technology, computers, and photography are among my many passions!

Currently, I'm actively engaged in various personal projects that bring me joy. These include website development, app creation, as well as photo and video editing. And of course being the leader of a big student organization named DEFI. We host social events and meaningful educational courses relevant to my particular study.

Outside of my academic pursuits and personal projects, I have a diverse range of activities that keep me busy. On any given day, you might find me exercising, immersing myself in video games, spending time with friends, or pursuing my love for photography.
